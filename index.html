How to Make Dalgona Coffee

Why I Chose Dalgona Coffee:

I chose this drink because whipped coffee is an easy drink to make that is inexpensive. It is delicious and has a strong flavor to it. Also, it is quick to make and can be paired with any type of milk. This drink became popular on Tiktok, so many people have made it and you can too!

Ingredients:

  • Milk (of any kind)
  • Coffee grounds
  • Sugar
  • Hot water
  • (Optional) Ice

Utensils:

  • Tablespoon measurer
  • Bowl
  • Mixer (electric one is recommended)
  • Cup
  • Spatula or Spoon
  • Straw
Allergy Info:
  • Dairy milk is included in this recipe. For those who are lactose intolerant, you can use milk alternatives.
  • Coffee grounds are used in this recipe. You may use alternatives, but the dalgona may not fluff up as much.
Step 1:

In a bowl, place 2 tablespoons of coffee grounds, sugar, and hot water.

Step 2:

Mix the ingredients with a handheld mixer until the dalgona is of a light-medium brown color. It should be fluffy.

Step 3:

Pour the milk into the glass cup. Any amount of milk is okay, but 8-12 oz is recommended. You can use ice if you want to.

Step 4:

With a spatula or spoon, scoop the dalgona out of the bowl into the glass of milk.

Step 5:

Drink with a straw and enjoy!
